Runner's Lust

Web BusinessPresent

My first business where sell expertly written training plans to help my customers reach their goals in the 5k, 10k, half-marathon, and marathon. Peruse the web store here.

Inspiration

As distance runner I have been lucky enough to enjoy a lot of success, earning 5 D1 First Team All-American honors, US Junior 5k Champion, 2016 5k PAC 12 Champion, becoming 1 of 500 Americans ever to break the 4 minute mile barrier, and becoming the owner for 4 new school records. Read my full bio here.

Through this crazy journey I have amassed a ton of expertise on training methodologies physiologies, and nutrition. Some of my friends have asked me to write training them for various road races. After awhile I realized I could monetize this into a relatively passive business in selling digital training programs on online.

Behind The Scenes

I named this company Runners Lust because I felt the quest for the runner's high and deeper self-awareness was similar to the Wanderlust quest for adventure and self-awareness. Check the Runner Lust web store here. I have recently got this site up and I am currently hosting it through Shopify. This has allowed to quickly create a MVP (minimum viable product) to run various split test with Google Adwords.

Once I feel the site and ads are moderately optimized and generating profit I will start developing my custom site for better SEO and control of my sales funnels. In conjunction I have also built a following of over 3 thousand followers on Instagram for my brand, follow me here you interested in adventurous and inspirational running posts.
