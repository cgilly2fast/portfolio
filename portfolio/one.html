I Only Achieved One Of These

Art InstallationOctober 2017

This piece embodies my mentality of accepting uncertainty and accepting failure as part of the process of achievement.

Artist Statment

The print is a poster I made that hung in my room from the summer of 2016 to the spring of 2017. This work is titled ‚ÄúI Only Achieved One Of These‚Äù because I only achieved one of the goals i put on I poster I made. The stack of papers on the pedestal are print outs from my training log from June 2016 to June 2017. If I completed the day of training, as planned by my coach, that days print out is placed in the stack in chronological order. June 2016 is at the bottom and May 2017 is on the top. If I did not complete a day of training, as planned by my coach, due to injury, fatigue, etc. that day is torn up around the pedestal.

Theme

This was the first work I have made about my running or just running in general. The sport of running has given me so much in my life and even bit of notoriety in some circles. What usually does not get seen is when I fail, the painful moments of falling short of your goal after spending months of extreme dedication. These moments are an integral part of the sport, or any sport, or any endeavor worth accomplishing you come up short time and time again.
